Dance forms of India

India boasts a rich and diverse range of dance forms,
with eight prominent ones recognized as classical
by the Sangeet Natak Akademi:
Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Kathakali, Sattriya, Manipuri, and Mohiniyattam.
These classical styles, along with numerous folk dances, reflect the cultural, spiritual,
and artistic heritage of different regions within India.
